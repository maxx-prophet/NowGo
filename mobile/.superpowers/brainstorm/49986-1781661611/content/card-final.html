Why the 2-row bar fixes the wrapping

OLD — flex-wrap
  • Long text shoves badge to new line
  • Leave-by moves unpredictably
  • Height varies card to card
  • Feed looks jagged
NEW — 2-row pill
  • Row 1 height: always fixed
  • Row 2 height: always fixed (1 line, ellipsis)
  • Badge always top-right
  • Leave-by always bottom-right
