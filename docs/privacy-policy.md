# NowGo Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 18, 2026**

NowGo ("we," "our," or "the app") helps you discover events happening tonight in New York City. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it's used, and the choices you have.

## Information We Collect

### Location
If you grant permission, NowGo uses your device's precise location to:
- Show you events near where you are
- Estimate travel time and tell you when to leave

Location data is used on your device to power these features and is sent to our servers only as coordinates alongside an event search request — it is not linked to your name or identity, and we do not use it for advertising or tracking. You can deny or revoke location access at any time in your device's Settings; the app will still work, just without distance/travel-time features.

### Usage Analytics
We use PostHog, a third-party analytics provider, to understand how people use the app — for example, which screens are viewed, which filters are used, and when errors occur. This includes:
- App interactions (screen views, button taps, filters applied)
- Your onboarding preferences (identity type, event interests, budget range) — sent to our analytics provider tied to an anonymous device ID, not to your name or email
- Crash and error reports

This data is used only to improve the app and is not sold to third parties or used for advertising.

### Locally Stored Preferences
Your onboarding choices (interests, budget, identity) are saved on your device so the app remembers your preferences between sessions. This local storage is not accessible to other apps.

### What We Don't Collect
NowGo does not require an account, and we do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or payment information. Ticket purchases happen entirely on the event organizer's or ticketing partner's website — we never see your payment details.

## Third-Party Services

- **PostHog** (analytics) — see [PostHog's privacy policy](https://posthog.com/privacy)
- **Apple Maps** — tapping "Directions" opens Apple Maps with the venue address; this is handled by Apple, not NowGo
- **Ticketing & event partners** — tapping "Get Tickets" takes you to an external site with its own privacy practices

## Data Retention & Your Choices

- You can disable location access at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy → Location Services
- You can request that we delete analytics data associated with your device by contacting us at **hello@nowgoapp.com**
- Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored preferences from your device

## Children's Privacy

NowGo is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date above.

## Contact
